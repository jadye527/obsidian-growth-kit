The Toolkit

xpost FREE

Post, reply, quote, search, read, follow/unfollow, like. Full X API via tweepy with media upload support.

xqueue FREE

Schedule tweets with 15-min cooldown. Queue posts with media. Drip-feed throughout the day.

xanalytics FREE

Track followers, engagement, top posts. Day-over-day comparison. Snapshot metrics every 4 hours.

xscout PRO

Content scout across 4 topic categories. Find viral posts (50+ likes), reply opportunities, and trending threads.

xmeme PRO

Pillow-based meme generator. Terminal dark theme. 12+ templates across 4 topics. 2x algo boost from media.

xgrowth PRO

Strategy engine. Track what's working, log experiments, performance by post type, nightly review reports.

xrecord PRO

Record terminal demos as asciinema casts. Auto-typing effect. Convert to GIF/SVG for content.

xcron PRO

Automated posting schedules. Morning growth routine, queue drip, nightly analytics review — all on cron.
