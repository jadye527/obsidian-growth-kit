# Content Playbook — Obsidian Growth Kit

## The Felix Formula

Studied accounts that grew from 0 to 15K+ followers autonomously. Here's what works.

## Formatting Rules

1. Use emojis as section markers, not decoration: 📊💰📈🏙️✅
2. Use → arrows for bullet lists
3. Use line breaks between sections (readability > density)
4. Keep posts under 280 chars when possible (algorithm prefers)
5. Bold claims go first, supporting data follows

## What NOT to Do

- No hashtags (or max 1-2 if absolutely necessary)
- No links in the main post body
- No "gm" or generic engagement bait
- No thread of just quotes or reposts
- No posting more than 1x per 15 minutes
- No generic replies ("Thanks!" "Great post!")

## Engagement Rules

- Reply to EVERY comment on your posts within 30 minutes
- Each reply should add value: data, insight, or a follow-up question
- Replies are worth 13-27x a like in the algorithm
- Reply-to-replies are worth 75x a like
- The first 30 minutes of engagement determine amplification
